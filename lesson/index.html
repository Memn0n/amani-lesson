Amani Foundation is a non-profitable Organization dedicated to assist
underprivileged people in various communities. We also provide
assistance with skills development and creating job opportunities for
mainly unemployed young people.

What We Do

CV Database Drive

A crucial initiative by Amani, Our goal is to empower unemployed youth by collecting their CVs and acting as a dedicated agency to assist them in their job search. We believe in the transformative power of employment and aim to bridge the gap between talented individuals and meaningful career opportunities. By actively applying on behalf of the candidates and providing personalized guidance and support throughout the process, we strive to enhance their chances of securing suitable employment. Join us in creating a brighter future for the youth by unleashing their potential and fostering economic empowerment through this impactful endeavor.
